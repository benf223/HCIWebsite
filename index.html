Introduction

As university students we use the regular mouse almost everyday. We have culturally mapped to the regular optical mouse as we have grown up our lives with it. Although, the regular mouse serves the purpose to navigate and interact with our computer the design of the ergonomic mouse is proven to be capable to do tasks that a regular mouse does.

An 3M ergonomic mouse is designed to support the wrist as it prevents anatomical position of the hand which is done when using a regular mouse. The image below represents the the arm placement between the ergonomic mouse and a regular mouse.

The user's mouse position has shown in research the discomfort on arm and hand placement. The ergonomic mouse design supports this by fulfilling usability standards such as

- More natural grip

- Assumes a natural wrist position

- Reduce of pins and needles and arm fatigue

Computers are always equipped with a mouse to allow us to to interact with the device. Even though we use computer shortcuts and the trackpad in our laptop the mouse always serves a essential purpose in completing tasks.
